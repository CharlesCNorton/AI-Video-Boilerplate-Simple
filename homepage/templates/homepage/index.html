AI Video Boilerplate

Mess with it at: https://github.com/DareFail/AI-Video-Boilerplate-Simple

List of Demos

General Object Detection - Detects objects in a video using Microsoft-Coco
Specialized Object Detection - Detects objects in any Trained Universe Model
Gaze Detection - Control a dot on the screen with just your eyes and a webcam
Priva-see - Pause and blur a playing youtube video when you look away from the screen.
